2. Bhutan

One of the happiest countries in the world, Bhutan offers free visa for Indians.
The country is known for its diverse landscape, wildlife, Buddhist temples, and trekking routes.

Visa details – No visa for 14 days.

Essential travel documents include passport and or a voter ID issued by the Government of India.–

Indians coming to Bhutan can collect the special permit at Paro international airport travelling by air Indo Bhutan border travelling by road.

The permit is valid only for tourists visiting Thimphu and Paro. For those travelling beyond them must take permission from RGoB Immigration Office at Thimphu
